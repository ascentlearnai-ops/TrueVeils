FAQ

Plain answers.

Does Truveil guarantee someone used AI?

No. It reports AI-assistance risk with evidence signals and human counter-signals. The interviewer makes the decision.

Does it truly block websites?

V1 uses detect, warn, refocus, and report. It does not kill processes, edit firewall rules, or claim browser-extension-level blocking.

Where does audio go?

Short chunks are uploaded for transcription and deleted immediately after processing. Reports contain transcripts and evidence only.
