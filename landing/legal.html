Trust & Legal

Built for reviewer-owned interview integrity.

Truveil is positioned as an evidence-first workflow, not an automated employment decision tool. The product is designed to help interviewers understand what happened during a session while preserving human oversight.

Review bands

  • Clear: no meaningful integrity evidence was recorded.
  • Review: events or transcript patterns deserve reviewer attention.
  • High-priority review: strong behavioral evidence, such as a known restricted AI destination, should be reviewed promptly.
  • Incomplete evidence: telemetry or transcript quality was insufficient for a confident review.

Product boundaries

  • No camera proctoring.
  • No screen recording.
  • No file or clipboard access.
  • No unsupported accuracy claims.
  • No automated pass/fail hiring decision.

Recommended customer launch checklist

  • Review these pages with counsel before selling to production customers.
  • Create a candidate-facing notice for each customer workflow.
  • Train interviewers to treat reports as evidence, not as a final verdict.
  • Document retention, deletion, support, and escalation procedures.
  • Test installer downloads, consent, transcription, event detection, report export, and deletion before every release.
