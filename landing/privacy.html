Privacy notice

Truveil is an evidence-first interview verification beta. With candidate consent, it may process live transcript text, foreground application names, window titles, browser URLs or hosts exposed by Windows, and restricted-target events.

Audio

Live audio is used for transcription. Temporary fallback audio must be deleted immediately after processing and is not included in reports.

Retention and deletion

Interview reports and transcript evidence are retained for up to 30 days by default. Recruiters can request earlier deletion through support.

Not collected

Truveil does not intentionally read personal files, clipboard contents, messages, or browser history.
